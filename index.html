Tilt 9 Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-22

Tilt 9 (the App) is provided by Home2Tech (we, us, or our). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Summary

  • Core gameplay works without creating a traditional account.
  • Optional sign-in is used for leaderboard features.
  • The App uses third-party services including Firebase, Google Play Games Services, and Google Mobile Ads.

Information We Collect

1) Leaderboard and gameplay data

When leaderboard features are used, the App may process and store:

  • Account identifier (such as Firebase UID)
  • Public display name (or a generated player label such as Player-XXXXXX)
  • Level number
  • Score values (time and moves)
  • Submission timestamps

Why we use it:

  • To save and display leaderboard rankings
  • To keep leaderboard entries accurate

2) Optional sign-in data

If you choose to sign in (for example with Google Sign-In or Google Play Games), the App may receive account profile information needed for authentication, such as display name and account identifiers.

Why we use it:

  • To identify leaderboard entries with a public player name
  • To support sign-in state for leaderboard submissions

3) Data stored on your device

The App stores limited data on your device, such as:

  • A local gamer name
  • A local queue of pending leaderboard submissions when network/service is unavailable

Why we use it:

  • To improve continuity of play and retry leaderboard submissions later

4) Advertising data

The App can show ads through Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Google and its partners may process device and advertising identifiers and related ad delivery data according to their policies.

Why we use it:

  • To deliver and measure ads

For more information, see Google Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

  • Run and maintain the App
  • Provide optional leaderboard services
  • Authenticate users for restricted features
  • Show ads and support app monetization
  • Detect and troubleshoot service issues

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

We share or process data with service providers only as needed to operate the App, including:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, database, and backend functions)
  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob)

Data Retention

  • Leaderboard records are retained to support ranking history and competition integrity unless removed through maintenance or support processes.
  • Locally stored queue and gamer-name data remain on your device until you clear app data, uninstall the App, or new values replace old values.

Your Choices

  • You can use core gameplay without optional sign-in features.
  • You may choose not to use leaderboard features.
  • You can clear local app data from device settings.
  • You can uninstall the App at any time.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards and rely on platform and service-provider security controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100 percent secure.

Children

Tilt 9 is not directed to children under 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law.

International Processing

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own through our service providers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the Effective date above.
